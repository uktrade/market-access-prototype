Photo voltaic panels

Country: United States
Sector: Energy equipment
Barrier type: Burdensome customs procedures

Consignments of photo voltaic panels from EU countries are being subject to particularly rigorous document inspection procedures at all ports of entry to the US. Onward transport has been delayed at San Francisco, New York, Delaware and Houston.

Why report the problem?

By reporting a trade barrier you can get the help you need, and also provide information which will help us to focus resources where they can be most effective.

Our team will review your report and respond to it within five days.

How it works

  1. Tell us about you/your role
  2. Describe the problem
  3. Select the trade barrier from a list
  4. Review next steps
  5. Provide additional information that may help us

We'll respect any needs you have for confidentiality, and we'll keep you updated on progress.

In an emergency

Request a fast track response to a trade barrier problem

Only make a fast track request if:

- you are facing significant and imminent financial or other loss
- you need action to be taken within the next 48 hours
