Example phone call text

If you call the fast track help number, you will hear the following message:

We will only be able to process calls which can demonstrate clear evidence that there will be significant and imminent financial or other loss, with action needing to be taken within 48 hours.

If your problem does not meet these criteria, please end this call and complete the online report a trade barrier form. A relevant expert will respond to you within five days.

Press 1 to stay on the line and report an emergency. Please have any supporting evidence to hand.

Press 2 or hang up to end the call.

Thank you.
