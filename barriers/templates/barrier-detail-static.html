Photo voltaic panels

Country: United States
Sector: Energy equipment
Barrier type: Burdensome customs procedures

Consignments of photo voltaic panels from EU countries are being subject to particularly rigorous document inspection procedures at all ports of entry to the US. Onward transport has been delayed at San Francisco, New York, Delaware and Houston.

While no reason has been given for this, it seems likely that officials are concerned about the possibility of products sourced in China being exported under an EU flag. In particular, officials are paying particular attention to document GB34, which lists the components in each product and their countries of origin. Any consignment with this document not fully completed and attested are at risk of severe delay.

Reference No: 3425A
Companies affected: Confidential, information available on request only
Status: Assessment
Actions: None at present
