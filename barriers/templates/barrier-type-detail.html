{% trans "Burdensome customs procedures" %}

Customs procedures vary widely from country to country, sometimes even from port to port. Exporters can be caught out by recent changes. Local officials can make demands based on their own interpretation of the rules. Be prepared! Our advice to exporters worried about getting their products through customs are:

  • Make sure you’re aware of the local customs requirements before you transport your products
  • Develop contacts in the country in advance, particularly people who speak the local language
  • Take advice from other companies who've exported successfully to the same destination
  • Make sure you’ve got the right insurance and are tracking your products in real time
