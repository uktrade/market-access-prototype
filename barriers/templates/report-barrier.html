The Government works with the World Trade Organisation and other governments around the world to reduce the impact of trade barriers. By reporting a trade barrier you can get help and you'll also be providing information which will enable us to focus our resources where they are most needed.

Use our service and you’ll be able to:

  • Get the right kind of help
  • Keep your information confidential
  • Get updates on progress
