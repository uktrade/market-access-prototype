{% trans "Find out about trade barriers" %}

When you export goods or services to another country you are protected by rules. They might be the regulations set out by the World Trade Organisation, or by the terms of a free trade agreement with the UK. It's important to find out about the rules that operate in your target market before you start. There's loads of useful information in our export country profiles.

Most of the time, the rules work well but sometimes there can be problems. Trade barriers are often the result of misunderstandings, misinterpretations or mistakes. Whatever the problem, teams from the Department for International Trade, the Foreign and Commonwealth Office and other departments stand ready to respond to UK exporters who report trade barriers.
